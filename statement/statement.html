Artist Statement

My personal works are influenced by many childhood memories and specific emotions that correlate into environment and expressions (or lack there of.) A lot of my work consists of character concept, sketches, and personal illustrations. Inspired by 3D media, animation, and traditional line drawings, I like to encorporate some sembalance of emotion through the use of lines, or express

The artists that I look for influence for my own works include: Soey Milk, Tatsuyuki Tanaka, Kuvshinov Ilya, and Grzegorz Kmin. Though these all focus more on illustrations, traditional and digital paintings, I look to them as an inspiration for composition, subject, mood, lines, and priority of emotions.
